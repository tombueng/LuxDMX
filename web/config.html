Web Serial isn't available in this browser. Use desktop Chrome or Edge (same as the web flasher). Safari, Firefox and mobile browsers can't talk to USB serial.
Plug the board in over USB, click Connect, and pick its serial port. The form below is built live from whatever the firmware exposes — nothing here is hard-coded, so it always matches your build. You can also set Wi-Fi here to get a stuck board back online. After saving, the device reboots to apply.
